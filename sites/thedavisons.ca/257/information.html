257 Maurice Road, Oakville

Welcome to this newly renovated main floor suite in Oakville.

The house features hardwood floors, quartz countertop and all new appliances. The bathroom and kitchen have been completely redone with high quality maaterials and workmanship. The house has a separate garage that is shared and can be used for storage as well as a huge back yard and patio where you can enjoy those warm days. There is ample parking and washer and dryer located in the basement

Schools, Parks and Recreation

This area is home to some of the best schools and just a block away a brand new community centre has been completed that includes a Hockey Arena, Basketball court, 2 baseball diamonds and much more.

Fine print

The utilities are not included, however, the landlord pays 15% of the total utilities to compensate for the basement suite tenant. Last year the monthly average for utilities was $325/month which includes electricity, water & gas. Internet is not included. Note that the basement suite does not have control over the thermostat. Lawn and snow removal are the shared responsibilities of the tenants. A lawn mower and shovels are supplied with the house. As this is a shared residence common courtesy and respect is expected between the tenants.
