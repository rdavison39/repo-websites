580K market

The 580K compete in the 14-foot backhoe and 50 to 70 horsepower market. Major competitors are the Caterpillar 416 and 426, Deer 310C and 410C, Ford 555B and 655A, JCB 1400B and 1550B, Massey 50H and 50HX and CDS 714C and 715C.

These competitors will be left in the dust by the Case 580K. The K was designed for the future and is years ahead of its competition in performance, serviceability, reliability and operator comfort

1990 Case 580K 4x4

I have a 1990 Case 580K 4WD looking for a new home. I bought it 5 years ago and the work I needed it for (clearing the land, trenching for power and building a septic bed) is now complete. I have put a lot of money into it over the 4 years - probably too much but it was a bit of a hobby to fix her up - changed all fluids, replaced driver side door ($1800 + shipping just for the door), replaced passenger window, various seals, installed new window panels in the back. Bought new battery and had the seal changed in the dipper and last year put in a new starter. Main boom cyclinder was removed and machine honed and then rebuilt ($1,800). Machines starts up every time - even after sitting idle for the whole winter. Just fired it up last weekend after sitting for 8 months with no issue at all. Turned the engine over for 3 or 4 seconds and it fired right up. Unit originally comes from South Carolina and as such as very little rust in the cab which is typical for machines of this vintage in the Canadian climate. Also installed hydraulic pressure gauge and you’ll see pump is strong and meets specifications outlined in manual. You won't find one in better shape as I searched for a couple of years before settling on this one. In the winter the machine was housed with a local mechanic who took care of all the maintenance for me that I didn't have the tools or know-how to perform.

The machine is located in Muskoka. If you wish to see it send me a note and we can setup a time - which will need to be on a weekend as its the only time I am up north. If you want a reliable machine this one is for you.
