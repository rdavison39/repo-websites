Tempest history

In 1972,Tempest built its first boat in the historic town of Caledon East, Ontario. The building wasn’t much - but the vision and dream was there. With a team of specialized technical and naval designers -Tempest set out to develop a line of high quality pleasure boats with an unyielding dedication to the principles that only the best is satisfactory

The small but dedicated Tempest team first began building the 153, quickly followed by the 147 - 156 - 166 and their flagship 178! It was to be the beginning of a long, hard but satisfying journey for a company wanting to make their mark in the boating business in Canada. The vision of growing continued and four new models were created - the TRX, 164, 190 and 227. Now, with a fully operational “production line” - it soon became apparent that the employees on the floor were true craftsmen - artists in fact, capable of building some of the finest boats in the industry - from concept and design to the cabinetry and fit and finish

Frequently Asked Questions

Are utilities included in the rent ? Utilities are not included in the rent for the main floor suite. The main floor tennant will be required to pay 75% of the Hydro, Water and Gas. The basement suite tenant has utilities included in the rent. Note: basement suite tenant does not have control to the thermostat but can regulate temperature by adjusting heating vents
How much are the monthly utilities ? Rates for gas and hydro electricity have flucuated over the last few years so its hard to predict precisely the price - i have history over the last 5 years and if you click on the link on the left called "Utilities Avg." you will see a chart that shows the average monthly payment for the main floor suite since 2007.
Is cable included ? Cable is not included. Typically, both parties from the main floor suite and from the basement suite make an agreement to share the expense of the cable.
How are the bills paid ? The landlord pays all the bills and collects 75% of the utilities (gas, water, electricity, hot water heater) from the main floor suite. As part of the monthly payments for the rent, the utilities is estimated and paid for. Landlord will keep track and if the amount exceeds or lags by more than $100 the parties will settle up. All bills are posted online and can be examined at any time.
What is process to rent the apartment ? The process to rent either apartment involves filling out a rental application form (see link on right side  under "Other info" for downloading rental appication form). Once approved then the first person to supply the lessor with the first month and last month rent cheque will secure the position to rent the apartment.
Is Smoking permitted ? No Smoking is permitted inside either apartments. The apartments are rented under this premise to people who may have allergies, small children or respitory problems and therefore it is not permitted to smoke anywhere inside the premises. This policy will be strictly enforced
Are Pets Permitted ? In general, no animals are allowed. Special exceptions may be permitted but you must have permission from the landlord in order to house any animals.
How is the Yard Maintenance handled ? Grass cutting and snow removal is a shared responsibility of the both the main floor and basement suite tenants. A lawn mower is supplied. Maintenance of the mower will be taken care of by the lessor. If the grass does not get cut, then the landlord will hire someone to cut the grass (approx $50 per cut) and will charge this cost back to the tenants
