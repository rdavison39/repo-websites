3 Mile Lake Luxury Cottage Profile

 

 Cute and cozy two bedroom, 776 sq ft cottage located on Hay Bay on the shores of Lake Huron. This cottage is conveniently located just 3 km from Tobermory. Located on a dead end road, this cottage offers a tranquil setting. The main living area of the cottage is an open concept with the dining room, kitchen and living room in one large open space. The sitting area includes a couch, chair, TV with VCR, CD and DVD player as well as an iPod/iPhone dock and a propane fireplace. The kitchen in the cottage is bright and inviting, thanks to the skylight. The fridge, stove, and microwave make preparing meals a breeze, and an island provides extra space for preparing meals. The highlight of the cottage is the sunroom. Floor to ceiling windows provide a crystal clear view of the nearby water, and the many comfortable seating areas make this a great room for enjoying a good book, or just enjoying the view. A table with four chairs provides the ideal place for a waterfront dining experience or a game of cards. Your family will love using this bright space. There are two bedrooms at the cottage. One has a double bed, the other has a set of single bunkbeds. The sunroom also has a sleeping space with a single bed. The one bathroom at the cottage is a four-piece with a bathtub/shower combination. Outside of the cottage is a large deck that overlooks the water and is a great place to unwind. Here you'll find a table and four chairs, the perfect place to sit and enjoy the view of Lake Huron. A key element of summer cooking is the BBQ, which you'll find on the deck along with a nearby picnic table for your outdoor summer dining. The shoreline at the cottage is honeycombed limestone, with shallow entry into the water and good swimming opportunities. Swim shoes are recommended. There are 2 sit on top kayaks with 2 adults life jackets provided for you to explore the beautiful shoreline of Lake Huron during your stay. The cottage is located just 3 km outside of Tobermory - a great, friendly community with a beautiful harbour, quaint shops and spots for you to eat-out before you go on a boat tour of Fathom Five National Marine Park.
