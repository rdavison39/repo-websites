205 Burnett Ave.

We puchased this house in 1992 and lived in it for over 5 years (in the basement) and did several improvements. In 2000 we renovated the first floor installing new kitchen cabinets and floor as well as re-sanded and stained the hardwood floors that are found throughout the first level.  The roof was replaced in 2000, the air conditioner replaced in 2009 and new furnace in 2012. I've installed a new garage door in 2014 as well as repaved the driveway and new interlocking stones in the front driveway portion and the back porch in 2014. I really take care of the place and expect the tenants to also take pride the house.
